Prologue: The First Echo
Before the NeuroVoid existed, before minds shattered like glass beneath the weight of forgotten guilt, there was a room. Small. Still. Silent.

In that room stood a single machine—cold, blinking, hungry. It was not born from malice, nor summoned by cruelty. It was forged from hope, from longing. And so they tried. But no one asked what the machine dreamed in return...
